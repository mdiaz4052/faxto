Invocation

Meaning is not found here.
It is declared.

FaXto is absurdist in spirit and liturgical in form: sacred grammar carried through electronic, country, operatic, glitch, rock, and machine-made sound. Each record confronts faith, doubt, power, compassion, and the silence between them.

On the machine

The machine is the instrument.
FaXto is the choosing.

Compositions, arrangements, and voices are generated with Suno from written direction and lyrics, then selected, revised, sequenced, and framed into finished records. The authorship is not hidden; the tension between human intention and synthetic production belongs inside the work.
