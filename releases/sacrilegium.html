story

This is my second official album. Where the first kept to a single palette, these songs move across genres — country, urban and pop, opera, glitch-hop, even bluegrass.

The lyrics reach toward religious and spiritual deconstruction, and restitution — questions, inquiries, and thoughts that have permeated my mind throughout my life; a representation of the burden of imposed beliefs. Its conclusion is open-ended on purpose, because my art is still a work in progress, and so are my beliefs…
