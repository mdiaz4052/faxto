This song is lyric- and story-heavy. A country-esque piece, it levels a heavy criticism at the notion of false equivalence — prompting the listener to think beyond binaries, and to contemplate the possibility that sometimes circumstances, good or bad, can exist on their own: without contrast, and without balance.
