story

This is the first official single. It reads — or sounds — like a love song at first, but the lyrics abstract the notion of irrational belief. The song was remixed and regenerated a couple of dozen times before running across this particular version (the one that ended up published). The earlier drafts were more electronic, but something about this “duet” version resonated perfectly — in its ambiguity and its spirit.
