DELUDED

Reflections that lie back.

Listen →
story

This is my first official single. It reads — or, I suppose, sounds — like a love song at first, but the lyrics abstract the notion of irrational belief. I remixed and regenerated it a couple of dozen times before running across this particular version, the one I ended up publishing. The earlier drafts were more electronic, but something about this “duet” version resonated perfectly — in its ambiguity and its spirit.
